Pcup

GOAL

creating customized illustrations for my client's products

THE CLIENT

PCUP srl is a joung and eco-friendly start-up. PCUP offers reusable cups, an alternative to disposable.

MY ROLE

Analysing my client's identity and creating original and customized illustrations.

RESULTS

Increased PCUP's products' appeal to users and made my client happy.

WORKFLOW

After their needs' study I ended-up drawing six different sea animals that represent the environment and the marine ecosystem. These animals completely describe the client's brand. Moreover, they represent the peculiar and unique characteristics of PCUP's cups:

The seahorse: a cheerful, fast and tireless animal. Because of his movements PCUP identified him with the discounts present in the PCUP app. Just as the seahorse is quick to reach rocks and algae to hang on with its prehensile tail, so those who use the PCUP app are active to take advantage of discounts and promotions presented by clubs and managers;

Just as the manta hovers with its broad wings over the seaweed and the fish in search of food, so users of PCUP's cup hovers over people waiting for a drink going directly to the barman waiting to serve it;

The jellyfish is the excellent example of the flexibility and portability of the cup. In fact, like jellyfish, PCUP glasses are soft, bend and do not have a rigid shape;

The butterfly fish represents the social side of PCUP. The desire to meet and see friends, to create new relationships and friendships;

The majesty of a hammerhead shark advancing in the ocean. Time, the wait that precedes the attack. PCUP's cups represent time too, waiting for a drink, enjoying a drink in its own time, without fear of it being ruined in a poor quality cup and without the ice melting inside;

The turtle is one of the animals that most end up in the spotlight due to the acute problem of plastics dispersed in the sea. For PCUP this animal represents their commitment to safeguarding the environment, nature and reducing the consumption of plastic;
