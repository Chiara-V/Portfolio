The Whole Bean - Landing Page

GOAL

Design a single web landing page for The Whole Bean coffee shop that will:

1. Attract as many customers to the coffee shop as possible during its grand opening weekend;

2. Form long-term relationships with potential customers, especially locals, so the coffee shop is top of mind.

Based on the business owner's goals, my design work has been focused around two key actions I wanted my users to take:

1. Attend the grand opening;

2. Sign up for email newsletter

THE CLIENT

Karla, the owner of The Whole Bean, is preparing for her coffee shop's grand opening. Her brand personality is cheerful, professional, easy-going, sincere and honest. She wants to promote a relaxing atmosphere.

MY ROLE

To design a single web landing page that will:

1. Announce the date and location of the grand opening;

2. Generate lots of buzz for the grand opening;

3. Introduce customers to the coffee shop and its products;

4. Encourage customers to sign up for the email list

RESULTS

Provided Karla with a landing page that reached a lot of potential clients and increased the number of subscribers to her newsletter.

WORKFLOW

I start by gathering information about what the goal of the landing page will be. Then I sketch some wireframes on paper, and design digitally the one I think is the most suitable.

I then create the final comp, adding details, such as colors, typography, images, icons, text.

This project is linked to "The Whole Bean coffee shop branding and identity design". As a matter of fact, all the design elements are used based on the brand style guide created for The Whole Bean.
