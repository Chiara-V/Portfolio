Sit Conmigo

GOALS

1. Showcase my client's chairs and entice customers to pre-order them.

2. Highlight my client's mission as a fair trade and fair labor designer.

3. Visually communicate the personality and mission of my client's business.

THE CLIENT

My client was Yolanda Lopez and her ethical, sustainable chair company, Sit Conmigo. Yolanda desired me to design a website to showcase her new chair designs and allow customers to start pre-ordering them. And of course, Yolanda wanted her site to be mobile-ready for all those smartphone shoppers!

MY ROLE

I designed from scratch all the site. The logo and the images were provided by the client. The deliverables I provided:

1. Mobile, tablet, and desktop design comps.

2. Color and type styles that are inspired by the Site Conmigo logo and mission.

3. Exported assets that will be used by Yolanda’s web developer.

RESULTS

Provided Yolanda with a well-structured online presence that respects her identity and business values. Answered to all her requests by creating a pre-order section, showcasing her products, highlighting her mission and visually communicating her personality.

WIREFRAME

In order to create a good design and layout, I started my workflow by sketching some paper wireframes. When I came up with the option that best answered my client's needs I drew it digitally. I designed the Sit Conmigo's site with a Mobile First approach, prioritizing how content and elements are displayed at the smallest screen size. My client's primary goal was to get customers to pre-order the chairs, so I designed the site keeping it ALWAYS in mind. However, I also focused my attention to all the other essential content, like the company’s mission and contact information.

STYLE TILE

I then studied my client's needs and selected the color palette and typography that best represented what the customer desired. Hence, for Yolanda's site I wanted the look and feel of the site to convey the values ​​of sustainability, quality, passion and care of the Sit Conmigo brand.

COLORS

1. Blue conveys feelings of trust and elegance;

2. Beige reflects Yolanda's reliability, and it also recalls the brand's ethical commitment;

3. Red expresses energy and stimulates the user to action, that's why I used it for the buttons and the navigation bar.

FONTS

All the selected fonts give a feeling of reliability, leaving room for the images and contents that are most important to Yolanda. They're all easily readable and well balanced, creating a pleasant user experience.
