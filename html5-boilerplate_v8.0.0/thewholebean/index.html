The Whole Bean

This project is linked to "The Whole Bean - Landing Page". As a matter of fact, after completing the brand style guide for the shop, Karla asked for a landing page.

GOAL

Design a brand for a new local business owner, Karla K. who was ready to open a coffee shop.

THE CLIENT

Karla needed me to help her develop a brand and identity for her coffee shop. She needed a brand that fitted her products and resonated within the neighborhood. Her product offerings include coffee drinks, a variety of loose leaf teas, and simple foods such as muffins, sandwiches, and granola bars. She'll also have some gluten-free and vegan options available. Karla would use the identity designs to create a website, printed menus, flyers, and business cards, and promotional coffee cups and t-shirts.

MY ROLE

The identity design deliverables I had to create were: 1. A color scheme 2. A typescale 3. A collection of brand images 4. A logo 5. An icon pair 6. A social media header 7. A brand style guide

RESULTS

Provided Karla with a clear and easy-to-read brand style guide.

WORKFLOW

The process I follow in order to create an authentic branding and identity design consists of different steps:

1. I analyse the brand's personality, and find out its main traits. In The Whole Bean case, the brand is sincere, honest, easy going, it's focused on quality and sustainability, and wants to promote a relaxed atmosphere;

2. Once I've defined the brand's personality, I indentify the brand's target audience. To reach this goal I study the customer's offerings, and narrow down the search util I find the brand's most enthusiastic fans;

3. I then write, always with the client, the mission statement;

4. I start focusing on the identity design, selecting the colors and fonts that best suits the personality of the brand;

5. Based on all the other steps I design the logo;

6. I select visuals and imagery that reflect the brand's personality, and that best engage the target audience;

7. I select or create custom icons (it depends on the previous steps and on the needs of the client;

8. I design social media headers;

❮ ❯

9. Putting together all the previous steps, I create a brand style guide ready to be used.
