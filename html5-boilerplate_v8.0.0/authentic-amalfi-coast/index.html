Authentic Amalfi Coast

GOAL

Increase users'engagement by creating a responsive layout, with particular attention to UI & UX elements.

THE CLIENT

The Amalfi Coast's Tourism Development Network helded a contest to re-design their site. The Network is made up of 48 private entities (hospitality, catering and agri-food operators, tourist services, mobility and handcraft products)

MY ROLE

Carefully analysing the project's brief I re-designed from scratch all the site, paying attention to UX and UI elements, working with a Mobile First approach. The logo was provided by the client.

RESULTS

Enhancement of the overall interface, making it easier for users to live an interesting and engaging experience on the site.

WORKFLOW

To create an authentic representation of the client's identity and to involve users, I selected all the design elements with care. First, I selected the fonts and colors that best represented my customer's values of quality, passion and sustainability.

FONTS

1. For the paragraphs I used PT Serif, which allows users to read easily. In fact, every content is clearly displayed and in harmony with the other fonts;

2. For the headings I opted for Roboto Condensed: readable, captivating and serious at the same time. Its simple, yet elegant character was perfect to convey feelings of reliability and usability;

3. The Alex Brush font was used sparingly to add a touch of personality to the layout, while allowing users to stay focused on the content. This font creates an authentic, unique and high quality atmosphere, in which users can recognize the Authentic Amalfi Coast values and mission. Though users' experience is still clear and simple. Ultimately, the contrast between the Alex Brush's curves and Roboto Condensed's lines provides a unique environment that inspire users to extend their stay on the site.

COLORS

The typography and color palette choices are firmly linked. The colors in fact support the personality of the brand and clearly convey its values: quality, attention to the environment and sustainability:

Yellow represents optimism, prompting the users closer to the brand. It also stimulates clarity and joy, so it creates a positive and engaging experience. Moreover, it encourages action, and that's why I've mainly used it for the buttons. This color involves users and helps them discover the different contents of the site. I employed yellow sparingly, as it was a support for users, and it shouldn't become a distraction.
I then used black and white, respectively useful to communicate space and elegance. To allow users to focus on content, I chose a limited color palette. So, photographs, fonts, and territory remain the focus of users' attention.

A balanced relationship between color, space, and typography is therefore created. This layout allows users to precisely recognize the Authentic Amalfi Coast brand, while enjoying a great experience on the site.

INTERFACE

The interface is designed to stimulate users' interest in the site and to be intuitive. In fact, they can clearly understand in all the site's spaces which actions they need to take. For an example, take a look at the newsletter's subscribe form:

Furthermore, during the experience on the site, the user has the opportunity to receive immediate feedback thanks the buttons, because they become darker once clicked:

I then used the typical User Interface patterns, such as:

1. The logo that links to the homepage, placed at the top left of the page;

2. The sample text in the fillable bar of the newsletter;

3. Slideshows and carousels to show photographs and some textual parts, in order to create an optimal use of space. Users can therefore appreciate the site at first glance and avoid scrolling too long (which would make them lose interest in the site).
