The Zen Garden

GOAL

Create different print designs & ensuring all designs delivered were on-brand

THE CLIENT

The Zen Garden is an Etsy Shop. Its owner sells a variety of digital printables: calendars, posters, bookmarks and more.

MY ROLE

Carefully analysing The Zen Garden's identity I created different printable items and a few social media headers.

RESULTS

Creation of different printable products. Enhancement of The Zen Garden's overall look and feel & of its offerings

WORKFLOW

To make sure all designs delivered were on-brand, I studied my client's needs and branding. I chose colors and typography that supported the client's personality traits of kindness, mindfulness, and I created a meditative and calm atmosphere.
