Blossom House

GOAL

Showcase my client's products and creating a user-friendly environment.

THE CLIENT

Blossom House is a creative flower shop. Its core values are quality, beauty and care. Since business has been growing, the owner needed a website that could showcase all the shop's products and, at the same time, allow customers to order flowers and plants online. One important request was to create a responsive layout, which included a Mobile APP design. In addition to it, my client had a KIOSK in his shop, so he needed a design for that device too, in order to make his customers interact with it.

MY ROLE

I designed a user-friendly interface and design from scratch, following my client's guidelines. In order to answer all his requests I specifically created different useful sections: a "product" section, a "shop" section, and a "gallery" section. To make it easy for users to navigate, I added a "search" section. Moreover, since my client wanted to write articles about his plants and flowers, he proposed me to create a "blog" page. I then included the "about" section. Finally, users can access to their personal account on the site, to see their previous purchases and to manage their orders.

RESULTS

Enhancement of the order management. Implementation of an online shop and of a user-friendly environment, where users have their own account section and can organize their purchases. Creation of a blog section that helps my customer to communicate with his clients.

STYLE TILE

The typography and color palette choices are firmly linked. They both represent the client's core values of quality, beauty and care.

COLORS

1. Green reflects growth, and it's connected to hearth and plants;

2. Yellow creates a positive and optimistic environment, which creates a nice environment for the user, surrounding him with clarity and warmth;

3. Purple express my client's creativity and his passion.

FONTS

The fonts support the personality of the brand:

1. Tangerine is used sparingly, to intrigue the users, without distracting them. It also express quality, beauty and creativity in a delicate way;

2. Gayathri is clear and easily readable, while creating a clean space and supporting all the elements around it: images, colors and the other font. It also reflect the client's reliability.
